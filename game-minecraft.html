Minecraft is a sandbox video game where players explore a blocky, procedurally-generated 3D world, discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and machines. About This Game

Minecraft offers endless possibilities — from building simple homes to designing complex machinery using redstone circuits. Explore vast biomes, venture into the Nether dimension, or challenge the Ender Dragon in The End. The game rewards creativity, exploration, and survival skills.

With regular free updates, Minecraft continues to expand with new mobs, blocks, and mechanics. The Java Edition supports thousands of community-made mods and custom maps, while Bedrock Edition enables cross-platform play across PC, console, and mobile devices.
