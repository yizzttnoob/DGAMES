About This Game

Counter-Strike 2 redefines competitive tactical shooters with its groundbreaking Source 2 engine. Experience dynamic smoke grenades that react to bullets, explosions, and move with wind currents. The new sub-tick system ensures your shots register exactly when you click, providing the most responsive gameplay ever in the series.

All your favorite maps have been rebuilt from the ground up with physically-based rendering and improved lighting. Classic modes like Competitive, Wingman, and Deathmatch return, alongside new gameplay innovations. CS2 also carries over your entire inventory from CS:GO, so all your skins and items come with you into the new era.
